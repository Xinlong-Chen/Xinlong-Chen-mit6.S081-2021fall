1. thread_switch needs to save/restore only the callee-save registers. Why?
   C语言函数调用会自动保存caller-save寄存器。

2. Why are there missing keys with 2 threads, but not with 1 thread?
   放置key的过程属于临界区，一个线程临界区没有意义。
   序列：
    thread A               thread A
    e->value = value;
                           e->value = value;
